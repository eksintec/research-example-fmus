Purpose of the FMU
==================

The estimation the carbon emissions is not trivial.
In fact, there are, depending on the context, different kinds and interpretations on how to do this best.

One part of the carbon emissions of a product are the emissions produced while the product is produced.
To estimate these emissions, a simple assumption is made:
the main power source (be it electrical, chemical, or whatever else) emits a certain amount of CO2 while generated or used.
Based on the energy flow rate (physically the power) and the emission density, the total carbon emission flow can be calculated.

For more convenient usage, a total energy measurement is implemented as well.
This allows to measure the energy used in a certain time span as well as the corresponding carbon emissions.
