Inputs and outputs
==================

The FMU has the following inputs and outputs:

Inputs
------

.. list-table::
    :header-rows: 1
    
    * - Symbol
      - Unit(s)
      - Description

    * - ``p``
      - W
      - Momentary power consumed by the plant under consideration.

    * - ``cMix``
      - kg / J
      - The mix factor :math:`c_{\text{Mix}}` defining the emission density in the primary energy mix.

    * - ``meas_start``
      - 
      - Flag to indicate the start of the measurement period. Positive edge triggers measurement start. If a measurement has already been taken, the value is automatically reset to zero prior to staring the actual measurement.

    * - ``meas_stop``
      - 
      - Flag to indicate the end of the measurement period. Positive edge triggers the finishing.


Outputs
-------

.. list-table::
    :header-rows: 1
    
    * - Symbol
      - Unit
      - Description

    * - ``dm_CO2``
      - kg / s
      - The momentary carbon emission flow caused by the plant operation.

    * - ``m_Total``
      - kg
      - The total mass of carbon dioxide emitted during the measurement.

    * - ``measurement_t_start``
      - s
      - The timestamp (since the simulation start) when the measurement started.

    * - ``measurement_t_stop``
      - s
      - The timestamp (since the simulation start) when the measurement ended.

    * - ``measurement_started``
      - 
      - Indicator if the measurement has been started in the past. 

    * - ``measurement_stopped``
      - 
      - Indicator if the measurement has been ended in the past.

    * - ``m_Meas``
      - kg
      - Internal integration value of emitted carbon dioxide.

    * - ``E_Tot``
      - J
      - The energy used during the measurement phase.
